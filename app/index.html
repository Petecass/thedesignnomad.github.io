Currently in Chicago, Illinois, USA

Life is too short to spend boxed up in one place.

Instead I want to live, breathe and experience as many new places and cultures as I can.

My bucketlist is ever expanding, and I try everyday to keep up. No complaints though; my insatiable wanderlust has me running around the world getting up to various (but thoroughly enjoyable) mischiefs - all the while funneling the insights from all I see and do into running my design studio, MELEWI with the help from my amazing team.

The world is my office, and it's impossible not to be inspired when you've the freedom to find yourself the best vantage point.
